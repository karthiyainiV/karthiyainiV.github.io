Amazon Prime Movie

The objective of this project was to analyze movies available on Amazon Prime within the time frame of 1952 to 2021, specifically examining their rating maturity level and language. By studying these aspects, we aimed to gain insights into the content available on the platform over the years and understand the trends and patterns in movie offerings.This project aimed to provide an overview of movies available on Amazon Prime between 1952 and 2021, focusing on their rating maturity level and language. By analyzing these aspects, we gained insights into the distribution of movies, identified trends over time, and highlighted popular movies within the dataset. The findings can be used to understand the content landscape of Amazon Prime and assist users in making informed decisions when choosing movies based on their preferences.
