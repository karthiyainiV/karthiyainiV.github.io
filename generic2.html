Yellow_taxi_dataset

In this data analytics project, I utilized Python to analyze the Yellow Taxi dataset. By calculating descriptive statistics, I gained valuable insights and summarized the data. Measures of central tendency such as mean, median, minimum, and maximum values were calculated for the 'total_amount' column. The standard deviation was also determined to understand the spread of the data. Moreover, I applied statistical techniques to identify outliers using Z-scores. By setting different thresholds, I examined the percentage of data points within one, two, and three standard deviations from the mean. Additionally, I utilized the scipy and statsmodels libraries to compute Z-scores and perform statistical operations. Through this project, I successfully visualized the data using graphs and tables, providing a comprehensive overview of the dataset. By applying data analytics techniques, I gained valuable insights and contributed to data-driven decision-making processes.
